About Me

Passionate about exploring the mysteries of nature, I've been captivated by science from a young age. My curiosity began with the allure of natural phenomena—rainbows, magnets, the intricacies of life—and expanded to deeper questions about life's origins, like Abiogenesis versus Panspermia. My academic journey at IISER provided the ideal foundation to dive into these compelling topics, and my current role at NIO allows me to further indulge in discussions on astrobiology and the possibilities of extraterrestrial life.


When I'm not researching, you can find me reading the latest science news, bike riding, or experimenting with new cooking recipes.

Current Focus
